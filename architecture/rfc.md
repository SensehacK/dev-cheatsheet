# RFC

## Intro

Request for comments, is a way of drafting, designing and finalizing a standard across an industry for wide adaptability of best practices and industry standard around the process of achieving some things.


## Reading

You can access public RFCs on [IETF website](https://datatracker.ietf.org/) 

They usually have a number designated to them and you can open or reference them with that.
